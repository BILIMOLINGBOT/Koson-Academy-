Present Continuous Inkor Shakli

Present Continuous zamonining inkor shakli ayni paytda davom etmayotgan harakatlarni, vaqtinchalik holatlarni yoki kelajakdagi bekor qilingan rejalarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi.

Yasalishi (Formation):

Present Continuous zamonining inkor shakli quyidagi formula boʻyicha yasaladi:

Ega (Subject) + to be (am, is, are) + not + Fe'l + -ing

Qisqartmalar (Contractions):

Koʻpincha inkor shaklida qisqartmalar ishlatiladi:

  • is not → isn't
  • are not → aren't
  • am not uchun alohida qisqartma yoʻq, lekin I'm not ishlatiladi.
Shaxs (Subject) "To Be" fe'li Inkor (not) Fe'l + -ing Qisqartma (Contraction) Misol (Example) Tarjima (Translation)
I am not playing I'm not I am not playing football. Men futbol oʻynamayapman.
You are not playing You aren't You are not playing football. Sen futbol oʻynamayapsan.
He is not playing He isn't He is not playing football. U (erkak) futbol oʻynamayapti.
She is not playing She isn't She is not playing football. U (ayol) futbol oʻynamayapti.
It is not raining It isn't It is not raining. Yomgʻir yogʻmayapti.
We are not playing We aren't We are not playing football. Biz futbol oʻynamayapmiz.
They are not playing They aren't They are not playing football. Ular futbol oʻynashmayapti.

Present Continuous Inkor Shaklining Vazifasi (Function of Negative Present Continuous):

Present Continuous zamonining inkor shakli quyidagi vazifalarni bajaradi:

  1. Ayni paytda sodir boʻlmayotgan harakatlarni ifodalash: Bu shakl gapirayotgan vaqtda davom etmayotgan, yaʼni hozir amalga oshirilmayotgan harakatlarni koʻrsatadi.

    Misol: I am not working right now.

    Misol: She isn't watching TV.

    Misol: They aren't listening to me.

  2. Vaqtinchalik vaziyatning inkorini ifodalash: Maʼlum bir davr mobaynida davom etayotgan, ammo hozirgi vaqtda amalga oshirilmayotgan vaziyatlarni koʻrsatadi.

    Misol: He isn't living in London this year.

    Misol: We aren't studying for the exam today.

  3. Kelajakdagi rejalashtirilgan, ammo bekor qilingan harakatlarni ifodalash (kamroq): Baʼzan, avval rejalashtirilgan, ammo keyinchalik bekor qilingan kelajakdagi harakatlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatilishi mumkin.

    Misol: I am not going to the party tonight.

Misollar bilan toʻliqroq:

  • The baby isn't sleeping.
  • We aren't having dinner at home tonight. We're going to a restaurant.
  • My friend isn't coming to the meeting. He is sick.
  • The weather isn't getting any better. It's still raining heavily.

Xulosa:

Present Continuous zamonining inkor shakli ayni damda sodir boʻlmayotgan, vaqtinchalik amalga oshirilmayotgan yoki bekor qilingan harakatlarni aniq va tushunarli ifodalash uchun muhim grammatik vositadir.

Umid qilamanki, bu professional darslik Present Continuous zamonining inkor shaklini toʻliq tushunishingizga yordam beradi. Agar yana savollaringiz boʻlsa, bemalol soʻrang!
