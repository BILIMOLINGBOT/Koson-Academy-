Present Perfect Negative Haqida Ma'lumot

Present Perfect negative zamoni oʻtmishda sodir boʻlmagan yoki hozirgi zamon bilan bogʻliq boʻlgan inkor etilgan harakatlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi.

Present Perfect Negative qanday tuziladi?

Present Perfect negativ gaplar quyidagi formula boʻyicha tuziladi:

Subject + have/has + not + Past Participle + (Object/Complement)

Yoki qisqartirilgan shaklda:

Subject + haven't/hasn't + Past Participle + (Object/Complement)

Qayerda:

  • Subject – Ega.
  • have – Yordamchi fe'l.
  • has – Yordamchi fe'l.
  • not – Inkor yuklamasi.
  • haven't – have + not qisqartmasi.
  • hasn't – has + not qisqartmasi.
  • Past Participle – Asosiy fe'lning oʻtgan zamon shakli.
  • (Object/Complement) – Toʻldiruvchi yoki holat (agar kerak boʻlsa).

Misollar:

I have not seen that movie. / I haven't seen that movie.

You have not done your homework. / You haven't done your homework.

He has not visited his grandparents. / He hasn't visited his grandparents.

She has not learned to play the piano. / She hasn't learned to play the piano.

It has not rained today. / It hasn't rained today.

We have not lived here for a long time. / We haven't lived here for a long time.

They have not traveled abroad this year. / They haven't traveled abroad this year.

Present Perfect Negative haqida nimalarni bilish kerak?

Present Perfect negativ gaplari haqida quyidagi asosiy narsalarni bilish zarur:

  • Oʻtmishda sodir boʻlmagan va hozirgi zamonga aloqador harakatlar: Inkor shakli oʻtmishda sodir boʻlmagan, lekin hozirgi zamonda taʼsiri yoki ahamiyati boʻlgan harakatlarni ifodalaydi.

    He hasn't finished his work, so he can't go out.

  • Hayot davomidagi inkor etilgan tajribalar: "Never" (hech qachon) soʻzi yordamida hayot davomida sodir boʻlmagan tajribalar ifodalanadi.

    I have never tasted spicy food.

    She has never been to a big city.

  • Oʻtmishda boshlangan va hozirgi kungacha davom etmayotgan holatlar: Bu shakl oʻtmishda boshlangan, lekin hozirgi vaqtda davom etmayotgan holatlar uchun ishlatiladi. Koʻpincha "for" (davomida) yoki "since" (dan beri) soʻzlari bilan birga keladi.

    We haven't seen them since last month.

    He hasn't played football for many years.

  • "Yet" soʻzi bilan ishlatilishi: "Yet" soʻzi koʻpincha Present Perfect negativ gaplarining oxirida "hali" maʼnosida keladi.

    They haven't arrived yet.

    Have you finished your book yet? - No, I haven't finished it yet.

  • Vaqt aniq koʻrsatilganda ishlatilmaydi: Xuddi pozitiv va soʻroq shakllarida boʻlgani kabi, agar gapda aniq oʻtgan zamon koʻrsatilgan boʻlsa (masalan, yesterday, last week, in 2020), unda Past Simple (oddiy oʻtgan zamon) negativ shakli ishlatiladi (didn't + asosiy fe'l).

    Notoʻgʻri: I have gone to the party last night.

    Toʻgʻri: I didn't go to the party last night.
