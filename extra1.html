A Day in My Life

I am a student. My name is Javlonbek. I am 20 years old. I am from Tashkent. My family is very kind. My father is a teacher and my mother is a nurse. I am happy because my friends are always with me.

Today is Monday. I am at home now. The weather is good. The sun is shining and the sky is blue. My little brother is playing in the garden. My cat is sleeping on the sofa. I am sitting at the table and I am writing in my notebook.

In the morning, I was very tired because yesterday I was studying until late. My breakfast is simple. I am eating bread and drinking tea. My sister is making breakfast for my parents. She is very kind and helpful.

I am going to school by bus. My friends are on the bus with me. They are talking about their homework. I am listening to music. My teacher is very strict but he is also very smart. We are learning English grammar today.

In the classroom, my friends are answering questions. I am sometimes nervous because the questions are difficult. My notebook is full of notes. The lesson is interesting and I am enjoying it. My pen is on the desk. The blackboard is clean.
