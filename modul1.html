🔹 to be fe'li qo'llanishi

Ingliz tilida eng muhim fe'l — 5 asosiy vazifa
1. Holatni bildirish
I am tired Men charchaganman
She is happy U xursand
2. Kasb yoki maqom
He is a teacher U o‘qituvchi
I am a student Men talabaman
3. Yoshni aytish
I am 20 years old Men 20 yoshdaman
4. Qayerda ekanini aytish
I am at home Men uydaman
They are in Tashkent Ular Toshkentda
5. Sifat bilan gap tuzish
The weather is cold Ob-havo sovuq
to be — am / is / are. Barcha misollar aynan shu fe'l yordamida tuzilgan.
