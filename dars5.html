Orqaga qaytish

"To Be" Fe'lining Inkor Shakli (Negative Form of "To Be")

Ingliz tilida "to be" fe'li (bo'lmoq, mavjud bo'lmoq) inkor shaklini yasash uchun uning asosiy shakllaridan (am, is, are) keyin "not" inkor yuklamasi qoʻshiladi.

Asosiy qoidalar:

  1. "Am" bilan:

    I + am + not

    Misol: I am not a doctor. (Men doktor emasman.)

  2. "Is" bilan:

    He/She/It + is + not

    Misol: He is not at home. (U uyda emas.)

    Misol: She is not happy. (U xursand emas.)

    Misol: It is not raining. (Yomg'ir yog'mayapti.)

  3. "Are" bilan:

    You/We/They + are + not

    Misol: You are not my friend. (Sen mening do'stim emassan.)

    Misol: We are not tired. (Biz charchagan emasmiz.)

    Misol: They are not playing. (Ular o'ynashmayapti.)

"To be" fe'lining inkor shakli qisqartmalari (Contractions of Negative "To Be")

Ko'pincha, gapirish va norasmiy yozishda "to be" fe'lining inkor shakli qisqartiriladi.

  1. "Am" bilan:

    I am not → I'm not (Bu yerda "am" qisqaradi, "not" emas)

    Misol: I'm not a teacher. (Men o'qituvchi emasman.)

  2. "Is" bilan:

    He/She/It is not → He/She/It isn't

    Misol: He isn't a student. (U talaba emas.)

    Misol: She isn't sleeping. (U uxlamayapti.)

    Misol: It isn't cold today. (Bugun sovuq emas.)

  3. "Are" bilan:

    You/We/They are not → You/We/They aren't

    Misol: You aren't late. (Sen kechikmading.)

    Misol: We aren't hungry. (Biz och emasmiz.)

    Misol: They aren't from here. (Ular bu yerdan emas.)

"To be" fe'lining inkor shakli vazifasi (Function of Negative "To Be")

"To be" fe'lining inkor shakli quyidagi vazifalarni bajaradi:

  1. Holatni inkor qilish: Biror narsaning yoki kimningdir qandaydir holatda emasligini bildiradi.

    Misol: The coffee is not hot. (Kofe issiq emas.)

    Misol: The shops aren't open yet. (Do'konlar hali ochilgani yo'q.)

  2. Shaxsni yoki narsani inkor qilish: Biror kim yoki narsaning kim yoki nima emasligini bildiradi.

    Misol: That isn't my car. (Bu mening mashinam emas.)

    Misol: They aren't doctors; they are nurses. (Ular doktor emaslar, ular hamshiralar.)

  3. Hozirgi davomli zamonda harakatning yo'qligini bildirish: Biror harakatning hozirgi vaqtda sodir bo'lmayotganini ko'rsatadi.

    Misol: She isn't working now. (U hozir ishlamayapti.)

    Misol: We aren't watching TV. (Biz televizor ko'rmayapmiz.)

Misollar bilan to'liq dars:

Men (I) am not I'm not tired. Men charchamaganman.
Sen (You) are not aren't a student. Sen talaba emassan.
U (He) is not isn't playing football. U futbol o'ynamayapti.
U (She) is not isn't happy about the news. U yangilikdan xursand emas.
U (It) is not isn't my problem. Bu mening muammom emas.
Biz (We) are not aren't going to the party tonight. Biz bugun kechqurun ziyofatga bormayapmiz.
Ular (They) are not aren't interested in this topic. Ular bu mavzuga qiziqishmayapti.

Umid qilamanki, bu toʻliq darslik "to be" fe'lining inkor shaklini tushunishingizga yordam beradi. Agar yana savollaringiz boʻlsa, bemalol soʻrang!
