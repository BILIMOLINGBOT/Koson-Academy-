"To Be" Fe'li Bilan Savollar (Questions with "To Be"): To'liq Ma'lumot

"To be" fe'li bilan savollar tuzish ingliz tilida juda oddiy va muhim grammatik qoidadir. Bu savollar odatda shaxsning, predmetning yoki vaziyatning holatini, kimligini, sifatini yoki joylashuvini so'rash uchun ishlatiladi.

"To Be" Fe'li Bilan Savollarning Tuzilishi:

Oddiy gaplarda "to be" fe'li egadan keyin keladi (masalan, You are happy.). Savol tuzish uchun esa, "to be" fe'li ega oldiga o'tkaziladi:

  • Tasdiq gap: You are happy. (Siz xursandsiz.)
  • So'roq gap: Are you happy? (Siz xursandmisiz?)

"To Be" Fe'li Bilan Tuzilgan Savollar Shakllari:

Shaxs (Subject) Present Simple Past Simple
I Am I...? Was I...?
You Are you...? Were you...?
He/She/It Is he/she/it...? Was he/she/it...?
We Are we...? Were we...?
You Are you...? Were you...?
They Are they...? Were they...?

"To Be" Fe'li Bilan Savollarning Vazifasi:

"To be" fe'li bilan tuzilgan savollarning asosiy vazifasi quyidagilardan iborat:

  1. Tasdiqlash yoki inkor etishni so'rash (Yes/No Questions): Bu turdagi savollar "ha" yoki "yo'q" javobini talab qiladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • Are you a doctor? (Siz doktormisiz?)
    • Is it raining? (Yomg'ir yog'ayaptimi?)
    • Were they late? (Ular kechikishdimi?)
  2. Ma'lumot so'rash (Information Questions - Wh- Questions): Bu savollar boshida who, what, where, when, why, how kabi so'roq so'zlarini oladi. So'roq so'zi "to be" fe'li va egadan oldin keladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • What is your name? (Ismingiz nima?)
    • Where are you from? (Qayerdansiz?)
    • How old are you? (Necha yoshdasiz?)
    • Why was he angry? (U nega jahli chiqqan edi?)
    • When is your birthday? (Tug'ilgan kuningiz qachon?)

"To Be" Fe'li Bilan Savollarni Ishlatish Mumkin Bo'lgan Vaziyatlar:

  1. Shaxsning kimligini so'rash:

    Misol uchun:

    • Are you the new teacher? (Siz yangi o'qituvchimisiz?)
    • Is he your brother? (U sizning akangizmi?)
  2. Predmetning nima ekanligini so'rash:

    Misol uchun:

    • Is this your bag? (Bu sizning sumkangizmi?)
    • What is that? (Bu nima?)
  3. Sifatlarni so'rash:

    Misol uchun:

    • Are you tired? (Charchadingizmi?)
    • Is the movie interesting? (Kino qiziqarlimi?)
  4. Joylashuvni so'rash:

    Misol uchun:

    • Where are they? (Ular qayerda?)
    • Is she at work? (U ishda emasmi?)
  5. Vaqtni so'rash:

    Misol uchun:

    • What time is it? (Hozir soat nechchi?)
    • Was the meeting yesterday? (Uchrashuv kechami edi?)
  6. Yoshni so'rash:

    Misol uchun:

    • How old is your sister? (Singlingiz necha yoshda?)
  7. Holatni so'rash:

    Misol uchun:

    • How are you? (Qalaysiz?)
    • Is everything okay? (Hamma narsa yaxshimi?)

"To Be" Fe'li Bilan Savollarni Ishlatish Mumkin Bo'lmagan Vaziyatlar:

"To be" fe'li har doim ham savol tuzish uchun ishlatilavermaydi. Agar gapda asosiy fe'l harakatni bildirsa (masalan, to eat, to play, to go), unda savol tuzish uchun odatda yordamchi fe'llar (do, does, did) ishlatiladi.

  • Noto'g'ri: Is you like pizza?
  • To'g'ri: Do you like pizza? (Sizga pizza yoqadimi?)
  • Noto'g'ri: Was she went to the park?
  • To'g'ri: Did she go to the park? (U parkga bordimi?)

Shuningdek, agar savol davomli zamonda tuzilayotgan bo'lsa, "to be" fe'li yordamchi fe'l vazifasida keladi va asosiy fe'lga "-ing" qo'shimchasi qo'shiladi:

  • Are you watching TV? (Siz televizor ko'rayapsizmi?)
  • Was he playing football? (U futbol o'ynayotgan edimi?)

Xulosa:

"To be" fe'li bilan savollar tuzish ingliz tilining muhim qismi bo'lib, ko'plab kundalik suhbatlarda qo'llaniladi. Savolning tuzilishini va qaysi vaziyatlarda ishlatilishini tushunish to'g'ri va ravon gapirish uchun zarurdir. Esda tutingki, agar gapda harakatni bildiruvchi boshqa fe'l bo'lsa, savol tuzish uchun "do" turkumidagi yordamchi fe'llardan foydalaniladi.

Umid qilaman, bu ma'lumot "to be" fe'li bilan savollar tuzish haqida to'liq tushuncha berdi. Agar sizda yana savollar bo'lsa, bemalol so'rashingiz mumkin!
