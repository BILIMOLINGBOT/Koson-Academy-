Past Continuousda Inkor Shaklini Yasash Haqida Toʻliq Maʼlumot

Past Continuous (Oʻtgan Davomli Zamon)da inkor shaklini yasash haqida toʻliq maʼlumot beraman:

Past Continuousda Inkor Shaklini Yasash

Past Continuousda inkor gaplarni yasash uchun "was not" yoki "were not" yordamchi feʼllaridan foydalanamiz. Koʻpincha ularning qisqartirilgan shakllari "wasn't" va "weren't" ishlatiladi.

Tuzilishi:

  • I / He / She / It + was not (wasn't) + feʼl + -ing
  • You / We / They + were not (weren't) + feʼl + -ing

Misollar:

  • I was not watching TV at 8 o'clock last night. / I wasn't watching TV at 8 o'clock last night. (Men kecha kechqurun soat 8da televizor koʻrayotganim yoʻq edi.)
  • He was not playing video games while she was studying. / He wasn't playing video games while she was studying. (U oʻqiyotgan paytida u video oʻyinlar oʻynamayotgan edi.)
  • They were not playing football because it was raining. / They weren't playing football because it was raining. (Yomgʻir yogʻayotgani uchun ular futbol oʻynamayotgan edilar.)
  • She was not cooking dinner when the guests arrived. / She wasn't cooking dinner when the guests arrived. (Mehmonlar kelganida u kechki ovqat tayyorlamayotgan edi.)

Past Continuous Inkor Shakli Haqida Nimalarni Bilish Kerak?

  • Yordamchi feʼllar "Was not" (wasn't) va "Were not" (weren't): Ega kishilik olmoshiga qarab toʻgʻri inkor shaklini tanlash muhimdir.
    • "Was not" (wasn't) - I, he, she, it bilan ishlatiladi.
    • "Were not" (weren't) - you, we, they bilan ishlatiladi.
  • Feʼlning "-ing" shakli: Inkor gapda asosiy feʼl "-ing" qoʻshimchasini saqlab qoladi.
  • Vaqt belgilari: Inkor gaplar ham Past Continuous bilan ishlatiladigan vaqt belgilari bilan kelishi mumkin (masalan, while, when, as, at a specific time yesterday/last night/etc.).

Past Continuousning inkor shakli oʻtmishda maʼlum bir vaqt davomida bajarilmayotgan harakatlarni yoki sodir boʻlmayotgan voqealarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi.
