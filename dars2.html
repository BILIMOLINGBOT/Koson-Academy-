"To Be" Feʼli Bilan Savollar

"To be" fe'li bilan savollar tuzish ingliz tilida juda oddiy va muhim grammatik qoidadir. Bu savollar odatda shaxsning, predmetning yoki vaziyatning holatini, kimligini, sifatini yoki joylashuvini so‘rash uchun ishlatiladi.

1. Tuzilishi

Oddiy gaplarda "to be" fe'li egadan keyin keladi (You are happy.), lekin savolda fe'l egadan oldinga o'tadi:

  • You are happy. (Siz xursandsiz.)
  • Are you happy? (Siz xursandmisiz?)

2. Savol Vazifalari

  1. Yes/No Questions:

    Are you a doctor? Is it raining? Were they late?

  2. Wh- Questions:

    What is your name? Where are you from? How old are you?

3. Savollar Qayerda Ishlatiladi?

  • Shaxsni so'rash: Are you the new teacher?
  • Predmetni so'rash: Is this your bag?
  • Sifatni so'rash: Are you tired?
  • Joylashuv: Where are they?
  • Vaqt: What time is it?
  • Yosh: How old is your sister?
  • Holat: How are you?

4. Ishlatilmaydigan Vaziyatlar

Agar asosiy fe'l harakat bildirsa (to go, to play), "to be" ishlatilmaydi.

  • Noto‘g‘ri: Is you like pizza?
  • To‘g‘ri: Do you like pizza?

Davomli zamonlarda esa "to be" yordamchi fe'l sifatida ishlatiladi:

  • Are you watching TV?
  • Was he playing football?

Xulosa

"To be" feʼli bilan savollar tuzish kundalik muloqotda eng ko‘p ishlatiladigan usullardan biridir. Grammatik tuzilmani to‘g‘ri tushunish sizga aniq va ishonchli ingliz tilida gapirish imkonini beradi.
