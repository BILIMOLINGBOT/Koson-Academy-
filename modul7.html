“To be” fe'lining salbiy shakli (Negative)

1. Holatni bildirish (State)
I am not tired. — Men charchagan emasman. She is not happy. — U xursand emas.

2. Kasb yoki maqom (Job / Status)
He is not a teacher. — U o‘qituvchi emas. I am not a student. — Men talaba emasman.

3. Yoshi (Age)
I am not 20 years old. — Men 20 yoshda emasman. He is not 30 years old. — U 30 yoshda emas.

4. Qayerda ekanini aytish (Place)
I am not at home. — Men uyda emasman. They are not in Tashkent. — Ular Toshkentda emas.

5. Sifat bilan gap (Adjective)
The weather is not cold. — Ob-havo sovuq emas. My phone is not new. — Mening telefonim yangi emas.
