"To Be" Feʼlining Qo‘llanilishi

"To be" ingliz tilining asosiy fe’llaridan biri bo‘lib, turli vaziyatlarda ishlatiladi. Quyida uning asosiy ishlatilish holatlari va misollar keltirilgan.

1. Shaxsni yoki predmetni aniqlash (Identification)

Biz kim yoki nima haqida gapirayotganimizni aytish uchun ishlatiladi.

I am a student. → Men talabaman.
She is my sister. → U mening singlim.
This is a book. → Bu kitob.

2. Sifatlarni ifodalash (Describing qualities)

Predmet yoki shaxsning xususiyatlarini tasvirlash uchun ishlatiladi.

He is tall. → U baland bo‘yli.
The weather is nice today. → Bugun ob-havo yaxshi.
They are happy. → Ular xursandlar.

3. Yashash joyini ko‘rsatish (Indicating location)

Kim yoki nima qayerda ekanligini bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.

I am at home. → Men uyda.
She is in the office. → U ofisda.
They are from Uzbekistan. → Ular O‘zbekistonlik.

4. Vaqtni ko‘rsatish (Indicating time)

Hozirgi yoki o‘tgan vaqt haqida ma’lumot berish uchun ishlatiladi.

It is 3 o'clock. → Soat 3.
Yesterday was Monday. → Kecha dushanba edi.

5. Yoshni aytish (Stating age)

Kimningdir necha yoshda ekanligini bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.

I am 25 years old. → Men 25 yoshdaman.
She is 10. → U 10 yoshda.

6. Kasbni aytish (Stating profession)

Kimningdir kasbi nima ekanligini bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.

He is a teacher. → U o‘qituvchi.
My father was an engineer. → Otam muhandis edi.

7. Yordamchi Fe'l sifatida (Auxiliary Verb)

Davomli zamonlar (Continuous Tenses) va majhul nisbat (Passive Voice)ni yasashda yordamchi fe‘l sifatida ishlatiladi.

She is studying English. → U ingliz tilini o‘rganyapti. (Present Continuous)
The letter was written by him. → Xat u tomonidan yozilgan edi. (Passive Voice)

Eslatma

"To be" fe’li ingliz tilining eng muhim elementlaridan biridir. Uni to‘g‘ri ishlatish uchun uning shakllari (am, is, are, was, were, been, being) va qo‘llanilish vaziyatlarini puxta o‘rganish zarur.

Umid qilaman, bu darslik sizga "to be" feʼli haqida to‘liq tushuncha berdi. Yana savollar bo‘lsa, bemalol yozing!
