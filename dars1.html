Orqaga Qaytish

"To Be" Fe'li: To'liq Ma'lumot va Qo'llanilishi

"To be" ingliz tilidagi eng muhim va eng ko'p ishlatiladigan fe'llardan biridir. Uning asosiy ma'nosi "mavjud bo'lmoq", "bo'lmoq" degan ma'nolarni anglatadi. "To be" fe'li turli xil vaziyatlarda ishlatilishi mumkin va u bir nechta shakllarga ega.

"To Be" Fe'lining Shakllari:

"To be" fe'li zamon va shaxsga qarab quyidagi shakllarga ega:

  • Present Simple (Hozirgi Oddiy Zamon):
    • I am (men)
    • You are (sen/siz)
    • He/She/It is (u)
    • We are (biz)
    • You are (sizlar)
    • They are (ular)
  • Past Simple (O'tgan Oddiy Zamon):
    • I was (men edim)
    • You were (sen/siz edingiz)
    • He/She/It was (u edi)
    • We were (biz edik)
    • You were (sizlar edingiz)
    • They were (ular edi)
  • Future Simple (Kelasi Oddiy Zamon):
    • I will be (men bo'laman)
    • You will be (sen/siz bo'lasan/bo'lasiz)
    • He/She/It will be (u bo'ladi)
    • We will be (biz bo'lamiz)
    • You will be (sizlar bo'lasizlar)
    • They will be (ular bo'ladilar)
  • Present Continuous (Hozirgi Davomli Zamon): Bu zamon "to be" fe'lining hozirgi shakllari (am, is, are) va asosiy fe'lga "-ing" qo'shimchasini qo'shish orqali yasaladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • I am watching (men tomosha qilyapman)
    • She is reading (u o'qiyapti)
    • They are playing (ular o'ynayaptilar)
  • Past Continuous (O'tgan Davomli Zamon): Bu zamon "to be" fe'lining o'tgan shakllari (was, were) va asosiy fe'lga "-ing" qo'shimchasini qo'shish orqali yasaladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • I was listening (men tinglayotgan edim)
    • He was sleeping (u uxlayotgan edi)
    • We were talking (biz gaplashayotgan edik)
  • Perfect Tenses (Tugatilgan Zam–ænlar): Perfect zamonlarda "to be" fe'li "been" shaklida ishlatiladi.

    Misol uchun Present Perfect Continuous:

    • I have been working (men ishlab kelyapman)
    • She has been studying (u o'qib kelyapti)
  • Passive Voice (Majhul Nisbat): Majhul nisbatda "to be" fe'li asosiy fe'lning uchinchi shakli (Past Participle) bilan birga keladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • The book is written (kitob yozilgan)
    • The car was washed (mashina yuvilgan edi)
  • Infinitive (Boshlang'ich Shakl): Fe'lning boshlang'ich shakli "to be" dir.

    Misol uchun:

    • I want to be a doctor. (Men doktor bo'lishni xohlayman.)
  • Gerund (Fe'lning Otlashgan Shakli): Fe'l otlashgan shaklda "-ing" qo'shimchasini oladi va ot vazifasida keladi. Bunda "to be" "being" shaklida bo'lishi mumkin.

    Misol uchun:

    • Being kind is important. (Mehribon bo'lish muhim.)

"To Be" Fe'lining Ishlatilish Vaziyatlari:

  1. Shaxsni yoki predmetni aniqlash (Identification): Biz kim yoki nima haqida gapirayotganimizni aytish uchun ishlatiladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • I am a student. (Men talabaman.)
    • She is my sister. (U mening singlim.)
    • This is a book. (Bu kitob.)
  2. Sifatlarni ifodalash (Describing qualities): Predmet yoki shaxsning xususiyatlarini tasvirlash uchun ishlatiladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • He is tall. (U baland bo'yli.)
    • The weather is nice today. (Bugun ob-havo yaxshi.)
    • They are happy. (Ular xursandlar.)
  3. Yashash joyini ko'rsatish (Indicating location): Kim yoki nima qayerda ekanligini bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • I am at home. (Men uyda edim.)
    • She is in the office. (U ofisda.)
    • They are from Uzbekistan. (Ular O'zbekistondan.)
  4. Vaqtni ko'rsatish (Indicating time): Hozirgi yoki o'tgan vaqt haqida ma'lumot berish uchun ishlatiladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • It is 3 o'clock. (Soat 3.)
    • Yesterday was Monday. (Kecha dushanba edi.)
  5. Yoshni aytish (Stating age): Kimningdir necha yoshda ekanligini bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • I am 25 years old. (Men 25 yoshdaman.)
    • She is 10. (U 10 yoshda.)
  6. Kasbni aytish (Stating profession): Kimningdir kasbi nima ekanligini bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • He is a teacher. (U o'qituvchi.)
    • My father was an engineer. (Otam muhandis edi.)
  7. Auxiliary Verb (Yordamchi Fe'l) sifatida: Davomli zamonlar (Continuous Tenses) va majhul nisbat (Passive Voice)ni yasashda yordamchi fe'l sifatida ishlatiladi.

    Misol uchun:

    • She is studying English. (U ingliz tilini o'rganyapti.) (Present Continuous)
    • The letter was written by him. (Xat u tomonidan yozilgan edi.) (Passive Voice)

Eslatma: "To be" fe'li ingliz tilining asosiy grammatik elementlaridan biri hisoblanadi. Uni to'g'ri ishlatish uchun uning turli shakllari va qo'llanilish vaziyatlarini yaxshilab o'rganish muhimdir.

Umid qilaman, bu darslik "to be" fe'li haqida to'liq tushuncha berdi. Agar sizda yana savollar bo'lsa, bemalol so'rashingiz mumkin!
